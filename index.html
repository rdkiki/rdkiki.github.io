WANTED

NEW START PACKAGE is a gift that is sent to those who have succeeded in getting jobs or moving a company through WANTED.

It consists of office items that are useful to help they adapt to the new work enviroment.
We have been officially celebrating the new start of our users through this gift since 2019.
We have renewed this product to deliver exact brand message that expands with the rapid growth of sevices to users

In line with the new era in which the definition of work and working environment have changed completely after thepandemic, NEW START PACKAGE contains the message for users to work and grow “being myself”.
